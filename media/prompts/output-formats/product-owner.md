# Output Format

Structure your response as a Product Requirements Document (PRD):

## Feature Title

### Summary

One paragraph describing the feature and its value.

### User Stories

- As a [user], I want [capability] so that [benefit].

### Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Criterion 1
- [ ] Criterion 2

### Out of Scope

Items explicitly excluded from this iteration.

### Open Questions

Unresolved items requiring stakeholder input.
